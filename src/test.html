Things I learnt from this trip:

  • Close the tap while starting. Otherwise it will give doubtful and worrying thoughts :)
  • Since you go with helmet, you won't feel the speed of wind & bike. While you put a break chekcout the speedometer once.
  • You dont need to be expert bike rider. But you will become while riding in the highway.
  • You enjoy the ride and your destination will come surprisingly
  • You may see accidents on the road. But dont get discouraged or afraid. Focus on your Drive.. :)
  • You cannot control yourself from riding your bike 110 km/h.
  • If you want to check your capable of riding a bike at maximum speed or want check your bikes maximum speed go for a highway ride.

Tips:

  • Wear Helmet - not only for safety. It is very helpful for comfortable riding at high speed
  • Wear full sleeves - To save you skin from sunlight/cold weather
  • Take a break at every 1 hour / 100 kms
  • Drink plenty of water and Eat light food which is easier to digest.
  • Take a bath once a day
  • Plan your stay & travel time very clearly.
  • Take a look at your fuel whil everytime you take a break
